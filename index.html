Programs

We have designed a table tennis program for people off all ages -children, juniors and adults from beginners to professionals, all year round, week days, Saturdays and During School vacations

Private & Semi Private Table Tennis Lessons

Our private and semi private lessons are individualized training sessions for those who want to improve their abilities faster, focusing on specific needs, whether these are basic techniques or advanced aspects of the game, like stroke development, match tactics and strategies which need more special attention.

Adult Group Table Tennis Lessons

The adult group lessons are the ideal choice for adults who love playing tennis, would like to improve their technical and tactical skills and who simply want to meet, socialize and enjoy unforgettable time with other tennis players. Our Adult Group Tennis Program is a comprehensive approach considering each individual’s ability, skill building and stroke development.

Junior Group Table Tennis Lessons

In our Junior Group Table Tennis program, students will enjoy learning the fundamentals of the game. Young players who are beginning to embrace the game will learn the basics in a safe and fun environment. Younger players who aspire to play professionally can further refine their skills and practice various techniques and strategies.

High Performance Table Tennis

Our High Performance Tennis Training Program is a personalized coaching, specifically designed for competitive young players seeking to play college and/or professional tennis. This is the player development system focusing on an individual player by taking a personal approach to meeting each player’s physical, technical and mental needs and reaching his/her maximum potential.
